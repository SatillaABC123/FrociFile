Put your video files in this folder with names like:
channel-01.mp4
channel-02.mp4
...
channel-60.mp4

The radio uses 10 front buttons and a tuning wheel.
Each wheel turn changes the active bank of 10 channels.

Notes:
- Video audio will still play normally.
- Video is shown in black and white on the old screen.
- The screen now has stronger glitch/static effects while tuning and while playing.
